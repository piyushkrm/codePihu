Snake-Water-Gun Game

A fun and interactive game to play with Snake, Water, and Gun!

Objective

The goal of this game is to choose between Snake, Water, or Gun and play against the computer. Snake beats Water, Water beats Gun, and Gun beats Snake.

Overview

In this game, players will select one of three options (Snake, Water, Gun). The computer randomly selects an option, and the winner is determined based on the game's rules.

Instructions

  • Select either Snake, Water, or Gun to play your move.
  • The computer will randomly select one of the three options.
  • The result will be displayed based on who wins.
